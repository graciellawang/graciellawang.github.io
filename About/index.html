公司简介 | About

象外寰中，寓意着超越表象去洞察事物本质，从更广阔的视野看待世界。
作为一家创意设计公司，我们致力于艺术，海洋环境保护与医疗健康领域。

  • 团队拥有多元化的的背景

艺术与理工交叉背景的创始人，机器学习与材料学研究博士，海洋与自然环境专家。

  • 全球使命

为全球文化传承，环境保护和人类健康事业留下持久的积极遗产。



XiàngWàiHuánZhōng signifies the pursuit of transcending superficial appearances to perceive the essence of things, and viewing the world from a broader perspective.
As a creative design company, we are dedicated to the fields of art, the ocean, and healthcare.

  • Our team boasts a diverse background

Founders with interdisciplinary expertise in art and engineering, PhDs in machine learning and materials science, and experts in ocean and environmental studies.

  • Global Mission

To leave a lasting, positive legacy for global cultural heritage, environmental conservation, and human health.
